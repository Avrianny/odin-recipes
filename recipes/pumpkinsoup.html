Pumpkin Soup

Summary

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1.2 kg / 2.4 lb pumpkin. Can use squash
  • 2 garlic cloves , peeled whole
  • 3 cups vegetable or chicken broth/stock
  • Salt and pepper

Directions
